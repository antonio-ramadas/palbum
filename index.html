Features

Effortless

Intuitive

Shortcuts

Full control without using a mouse

How to use

1. Listen to Spotify wherever you want

2. Check all albums/playlists you listened before

The track that appear under the album is the last one you heard

3. Resume listening from where you left off

Select the album/playlist you want with your mouse or keyboard
